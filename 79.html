da Vinci, L. (1495–1498). The Last Supper [Tempera and oil on plaster].

Leonardo da Vinci’s The Last Supper masterfully orchestrates rhythm and movement through the expressive gestures and groupings of the apostles, whose reactions ripple outward from Christ’s serene center. The orthogonal lines of the ceiling and walls, converging at the vanishing point behind Christ’s head, generate a powerful linear flow that anchors the viewer’s gaze and reinforces his spiritual centrality within the scene.
