Balla, G. (1912). Dynamism of a Dog on a Leash [Oil on canvas].

In Dynamism of a Dog on a Leash, Balla uses rhythmic repetition and layered motion to depict the rapid movement of legs, tail, and leash. The overlapping forms create a visual stutter that captures the energy and tempo of a brisk walk, embodying the Futurist obsession with speed and mechanized motion.
