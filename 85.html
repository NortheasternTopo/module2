Duchamp, M. (1912). Nude Descending a Staircase, No. 2 [Oil on canvas].

In Nude Descending a Staircase, Marcel Duchamp breaks the figure into overlapping fragments to convey the sequential rhythm of movement through time and space. The repeated, mechanistic forms create a pulsating flow that evokes both motion and momentum, merging Cubist structure with Futurist energy.
