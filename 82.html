Marinetti, F. (1919). A Tumultuous Assembly. Numerical Sensibility [Letterpress].

In A Tumultuous Assembly. Numerical Sensibility, Marinetti uses rhythmic clusters of type, shifting scales, and angled arrangements to evoke speed, chaos, and sonic energy. The dynamic composition rejects linear order in favor of explosive movement, capturing the restless spirit of Futurism through visual noise and kinetic force.
