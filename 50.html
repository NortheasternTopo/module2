Barragán, L. (1947). Casa Estudio Luis Barragán (vestibule)

In the vestibule of Barragán’s Casa Estudio, contrast is heightened through the interplay of shadow and bold color blocks—especially the vibrant yellow and pink against neutral walls. Strategic use of light and geometry creates a clear visual hierarchy, guiding the viewer’s gaze upward and inward, while emphasizing the spatial drama of the architectural composition.
