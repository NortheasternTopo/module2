Kastel, R. (1974). Jaws [Offset print].

Roger Kastel’s 1974 Jaws poster uses dramatic contrast in both scale and color to heighten tension and direct the viewer’s attention. The immense shark rising from the deep dwarfs the lone swimmer, creating a clear visual hierarchy that emphasizes her vulnerability. The cool blue tones of the water clash with the bold red title, reinforcing the threat, while the shark’s upward trajectory underscores the immediacy of danger lurking just below the surface.
