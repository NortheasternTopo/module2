Petyarre, G. (2009). Bush Medicine Leaves [Synthetic polymer on Belgian linen].

In Bush Medicine Leaves, Gloria Petyarre uses rhythmic repetition and flowing lines to evoke the natural motion of leaves stirred by the wind. The layered brushstrokes generate a pulsing visual energy, creating a sense of movement that reflects both the vitality of the land and the healing traditions of her Anmatyerre culture.
