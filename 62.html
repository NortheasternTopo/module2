Klimt, G. (1905–1909). Tree of Life [Mosaic mural].

In Tree of Life, Klimt uses the repetition of spiraling branches and decorative motifs to create a rhythmic, dreamlike pattern that fills the canvas with symbolic energy. Though organic and asymmetrical, the composition is anchored by a balanced alignment of figures and forms, merging natural growth with ornamental design in a unified visual tapestry.
