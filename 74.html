Biesele, I. (1956). Eurax – Geigy AG. [Offset print].

Igildo Biesele’s poster for Eurax combines the clinical precision of the International Typographic Style with expressive repetition in the brush-like textures that evoke the sensation of scratching. The stark alignment of the sans-serif text contrasts with the chaotic yet patterned overlay of marks, creating a striking tension between order and irritation—perfectly reflecting the product’s purpose.
