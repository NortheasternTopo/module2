af Klint, H. (1907). The Ten Largest [Gouache and tempera on paper, mounted on canvas].

Seen together in this immersive installation, The Ten Largest becomes a unified environment of vibrant variety and symbolic harmony. The monumental scale, curved arrangement, and rhythmic interplay of shapes and colors envelop the viewer in a coherent yet dynamic visual experience, reinforcing the work’s exploration of life’s stages as an interconnected spiritual continuum.
