Gamboa Naon, M. (2018). Bbccclll [Generative Processing code].

Manolo Gamboa Naon’s Bbccclll explores rhythm, contrast, and movement through a generative composition of overlapping geometric forms and vibrant transparencies. Guided by code, the visual principles serve as flexible constraints, allowing for infinite variation within a defined aesthetic system—demonstrating how structure and spontaneity can coexist in algorithmic art.
