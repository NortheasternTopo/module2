van de Velde, H. (1898). Tropon [Offset print].

Henry van de Velde’s Tropon poster, designed for a protein-based egg product, translates organic motion into a stylized industrial language. The repeating, yolk-like forms sweep across the surface in tightly choreographed arcs, while the sinuous linework undulates with a fluid, almost musical rhythm. Deeply influenced by Japanese prints such as Hokusai’s, van de Velde’s Art Nouveau sensibility transforms a commercial advertisement into a lyrical composition of visual cadence.
