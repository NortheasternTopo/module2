Bramante, D., Michelangelo, B., Maderno, C., & Bernini, G. (1506–1626). St. Peter's Basilica

Inside St. Peter's Basilica, the soaring vaults, massive columns and intricate ornamentation envelop the individual, casting them as a small part of a larger religious cosmos. Here, proportion is used to humble and inspire reverence, placing divine power at the center of architectural experience.
