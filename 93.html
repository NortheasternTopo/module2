Müller-Brockmann, J. (1958). Tonhalle Quartett [Offset print].

Josef Müller-Brockmann’s Tonhalle Quartett poster uses repetition and alignment of geometric forms to create a calm, measured rhythm that reflects the structure of classical music. The overlapping circles generate a subtle visual cadence, while the asymmetrical grid and clean typography reinforce a sense of dynamic balance and forward movement.
