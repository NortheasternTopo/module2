Müller-Brockmann, J. (1960). Musica Viva [Offset print].

Josef Müller-Brockmann’s Musica Viva poster uses repetition, alignment, and pattern to evoke the structure and rhythm of classical music through a series of concentric arcs in red, yellow, and green. While the circular forms suggest harmonic complexity and layered orchestration, the unorthodox diagonal alignment of the red concert text introduces a subtle counterpoint—adding visual tension to an otherwise highly ordered design.
