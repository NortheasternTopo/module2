Beggarstaff, J. and W. (1894). Hamlet [Lithograph].

The Beggarstaff Brothers’ 1894 Hamlet poster uses stark contrast between flat black shapes and a muted brown background to create immediate visual emphasis. The simplified silhouette and minimalist palette heighten the drama of the scene, while the juxtaposition of Hamlet’s living figure and the pale skull in his hands creates a striking contrast between life and death. This dynamic establishes a clear visual hierarchy that draws the viewer’s focus to the existential tension at the heart of the play.
