Barragán, L. (1977). Casa Gilargi

Luis Barragán’s yellow hallway in Casa Gilargi masterfully uses contrast, emphasis, and hierarchy to guide the viewer’s experience. The saturated yellow walls and rhythmic shadows establish a dominant visual field, while the sudden burst of blue and red at the hallway’s end creates a focal point that draws the eye and signals a transition—both spatial and emotional—within the architectural journey.
